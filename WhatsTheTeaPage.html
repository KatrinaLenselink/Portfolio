What's the Tea

This is What's the Tea. It was a group project done in a group of three. I worked on the main page, find location page, and the community page. The purpose of this website is to connect users and help them to find restaurants and cafes with the best environmental practices.

The Process


Wireframe stage

Our main focus when designing the wireframe was to keep it simplistic and user-friendly. We placed the navigation bar along the top so that it would be easily accessible but not in the way of users.


Figma design stage

We chose the green colour palette as we wanted to focus on the environment and natural tones. We kept our Figma designs similar to the initial wireframes and added a few more pages.


Final Website stage

The final website had to be an HTML website so my team needed to adapt our inital designs to fit the layout and space of a desktop. We tried to keep the layout as similar as possible and mostly expanded the content to fill the space. We feel the desktop application felt less cluttered and was a better user experience compared to the mobile design.
