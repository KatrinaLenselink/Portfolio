The Process


Original Website

This is the original Goodreads website layout. The reason I decided to change this website was because I felt the original page had too much information which made it confusing for the user.


Figma Design Stage

This is the first redesign of the Goodreads website that I created. My original design idea was to make it feel more cozy with warmer colours and a bookshelf vibe. I changed the organization of the categories to make it easier to read and find what you're looking for.


Second Figma Page

This is my second redesign of the website, using feedback I recieved on the original submission. The feedback was to lighten up the colours and use more transparency to make the page feel less dense and enclosed. My new colour palette includes light blues and browns to incorporate the airy vibe without losing colours that remind me of books.
